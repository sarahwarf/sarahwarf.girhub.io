Faculty Development Workshops

I offer workshops and invited talks for university teachers that can be delivered as individual sessions, short workshop series, or full professional development programs, either online or in person. These sessions are designed for instructors working in EFL, EMI, and academic English contexts and focus on practical strategies teachers can apply directly in their classrooms.

My sessions generally fall into two broad areas: AI-integrated teaching and language pedagogy. Universities and departments often request a specific topic, and sessions can be adapted to local needs. These sessions were originally developed through faculty development work with universities in China and through presentations at regional and international conferences. They combine classroom experience, research-informed pedagogy, and hands-on experimentation so that teachers leave with strategies they can immediately apply in their own courses.
